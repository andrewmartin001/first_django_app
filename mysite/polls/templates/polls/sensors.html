Software for smart sensors

Algorithms for smart measuring devices

Overview

I have worked within Electronics Research to develop Bayesian algorithms for measurement devices that work in challenging environments.

Walk-over-weighing

The main effort is in weighing cattle as they walk over a platform. The major problems are that the cattle are moving and that often several animals are on the platform at once. I designed and implemented the algorithms that can weigh walking cattle, even if they "overlap" on the weighbridge. I managed to escape appearing in the video which illustrates our work:

We have made an open source agri-tech database, agbase.

GPS

I have also been part of the GPS project. We designed algorithms for improved GPS measurements and real-time uncertainty quantification.
